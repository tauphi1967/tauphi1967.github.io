About Tau Phi

Tau Phi Fraternity is rooted in tradition and driven by a strong sense of brotherhood, pride, and service. Since 1967, the fraternity has represented lasting bonds, leadership, and a culture that continues to shape generations.

Our Legacy

Tau Phi has built a reputation centered around loyalty, character, and lasting tradition. From campus involvement to alumni connection, the fraternity continues to represent a strong and unified brotherhood.


With a culture shaped by shared experiences and pride in its history, Tau Phi remains committed to building men who lead with confidence, purpose, and respect.

Brotherhood & Values

Everything Tau Phi stands for is built on a few core principles that define the culture and identity of the fraternity.

Brotherhood

Strong bonds, shared loyalty, and lifelong relationships are at the heart of Tau Phi.

Leadership

Members are encouraged to lead with integrity, confidence, and a commitment to excellence.

Tradition

Tau Phi honors its history while continuing to build a legacy that lasts beyond college.

Pride

Pride in the letters, the brotherhood, and the standards of Tau Phi defines the experience.
