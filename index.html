About ΤΦ

Tau Phi Fraternity is a brotherhood rooted in tradition, pride, and lifelong connection. Since 1967, Tau Phi has worked to build a strong community centered around friendship, leadership, and involvement.

Located in Nashville, Tennessee, Tau Phi continues to bring members together through campus traditions, social events, philanthropy, service opportunities, and a shared commitment to representing the fraternity well.

From brotherhood events to philanthropy efforts and community involvement, ΤΦ stands for more than just history. It stands for loyalty, service, and a legacy that carries forward through every generation.

Tradition, Brotherhood, and Philanthropy

Tau Phi is built on strong traditions and memorable events that bring the fraternity together. These experiences help shape the culture of the organization and strengthen the bond between members.

Philanthropy and service are also an important part of ΤΦ. Through service projects, community support, and philanthropy events, the fraternity works to make a positive impact beyond campus.

Whether through social events, service work, or fraternity traditions, Tau Phi continues to create a brotherhood experience that is meaningful, lasting, and rooted in purpose.
